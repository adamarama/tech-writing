# Troubleshooting Playback Errors

## Overview

Playback issues can stem from connectivity problems, device compatibility, or temporary service interruptions. If you get the following error message, use this article to troubleshoot the problem:

```Cannot play the selected title. Please try again later.```

---

### 1. Check Your Internet Connection

Streaming requires a stable internet connection. Test and verify that your device is connected to the internet using the following methods:

- Restart your modem and router.
- Run a speed test (recommended: at least 5 Mbps for HD video, 25 Mbps for 4K video).
- Try switching between Wi-Fi and a wired connection.

### 2. Refresh or Restart the App

Temporary errors or glitches can often be resolved by restarting the app.

- Close and reopen the app.
- Log out and log back into your account.
- Restart your device completely.

### 3. Update the App or Device

Outdated software or device drivers can potentially cause compatibility issues.

- Check for app updates in your device's app store.
- Ensure your device’s operating system is up to date.

### 4. Clear the Device Cache

Your device may have corrupted files stored in its cache that can interfere with playback.
**NOTE: Clearing the cache and data will log you out of the app.**

1. Go to your device settings.
2. Find the app name in the list.
3. Clear cache.

### 5. Try a Different Device or Browser

This helps identify whether the issue is device-specific.

- Switch to another phone, tablet, or computer.
- If using a browser, try a different one, such as Chrome, Safari, or Edge.

### 6. Disable VPN or Proxy Services
Virtual private networks (VPNs) or proxies can interfere with streaming access.

- Turn off any VPN or proxy connections before retrying playback.

### 7. Check for Service Outages

In rare cases, the issue may be due to a service outage on our end.

- Visit the service status page
- Check official social media channels for updates

## Still Having Issues?

If none of the above steps resolves the problem, contact our support team with the following details:

- Error message or code (if shown)
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Steps you’ve already tried

Providing this information can help us resolve your issue more quickly.

## Tips for Best Performance

- Use a strong, stable internet connection
- Keep your app and device updated
- Avoid running too many apps simultaneously
